The Hunt for Vehicle Theft Predictors
We were asked to find a data set where we could apply regression analysis using Excel to identify significant predictors. Our group obtained statistics from the U.S. Census Bureau. We obtained demographic data from 1981 to 2008 and vehicle theft data from 2001-2016. We used the overlap time period of 2001-2008 to conduct our analysis. We used the census data to identify statistically significant predictors to predict future vehicle thefts. We used Microsoft Excel to create scatter plots and created a regression model based on our data. Our resulting model aligned well with our data.
Software Used: Microsoft Excel (PivotTables, VBA)
